Carbonara

Description

A rich mix of crunchy guanciale and luscious, delicate cheese and egg cream.

This recipe looks simple, but it's actually quite tricky to pull off perfectly.

Ingredients for one:

  • 200 gr. spaghetti
  • 1 whole egg + 1 yolk
  • 20-30 gr pecorino cheese
  • 80 gr. guanciale
  • lots of ground pepper

Steps

  1. Cut guanciale into 1/2 cm strips and fry it on low heat
  2. Boil salted water for pasta
  3. Beat eggs, cheese and pepper in a small bowl
  4. Remove guanciale from the pan leaving the rendered fat in the pan
  5. Cook spaghetti al dente and put them in the pan where we cooked the guanciale
  6. Toss the pasta adding starchy water until the pasta is the right consistency and a cream formed
  7. Add a spoonful of hot starchy water into the egg mixture to help melting the cheese
  8. Add cheese mixture to pasta at the lowest heat and toss until the whole thing becomes super creamy (adding starchy water if it gets too dry)
