Roasted potatoes

Description

This recipe will give you the crunchiest, fluffiest roasted potatoes.

Ingredients for 4:

  • 2 kg. potatoes
  • 30gr. duck fat
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 liters water

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them in 2-3 cm cubes
  2. Preheat your oven at 200Â° C with convection
  3. Boil them in a pot with salt and baking powder for about 6 minutes
  4. Drain them and let them steam
  5. Put them in a bowl with some salt and the fat and toss them vigorously
  6. Roast the potatoes for 20 minutes then flip them and let them go for another 20 minutes or until golden brown
