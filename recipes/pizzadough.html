Pizza dough

Description

Making dough at home is quite easy, and it's 10 times better than store bought one!

Ingredients for 4 pizza balls:

  • 575 gr. type 1, pizza or bread flour
  • 400 ml. cold water
  • 3 gr. yeast
  • 12 gr. salt
  • 12 gr. olive oil

Steps

  1. Fill a large bowl with water
  2. Put half of the flour and mix until combined
  3. Add yeast, salt, oil and the remaining flour
  4. Mix until all the flour has been absorbed
  5. Cover and let it rest 20 minutes
  6. Wet your hands and stretch and fold the dough until it becomes smoother
  7. Let it rest for 30 minutes
  8. Repeat 6-7 step for 4-5 times or until the dough has become very stretchy and not stick
  9. Cover and let it rise for 3-4 hours
  10. Put it in the fridge for a night
  11. Divide the dough in 4 and put them in some oiled containers and consume them within 5 days.
